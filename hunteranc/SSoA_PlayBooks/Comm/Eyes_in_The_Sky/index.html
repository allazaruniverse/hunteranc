Eyes in The Sky(INT/DEX)

"Why fight on the ground when you can haunt it from above?"

"You see a battlefield; I see a chessboard where I’m always three moves ahead."

Overview

The Eyes in the Sky is the team's aerial scout, surveillance expert, and precision attacker, utilizing intellect and dexterity to maintain tactical superiority. Whether through sniping, drone operations, or securing high vantage points, they excel at gathering intelligence, making precision strikes, and controlling the battlefield from above. Their expertise ensures the team stays one step ahead of the target by providing critical reconnaissance and strategic firepower.

A fusion of sniper and surveillance operator, The Eyes in the Sky engages targets unseen while delivering vital field intelligence. They specialize in precision attacks, drone deployment, and reconnaissance, ensuring the team operates with superior knowledge and positioning. Their role is to provide cover, track target movements, and eliminate threats from a distance, making them an indispensable asset in any Hunt.

The Eyes in the Sky excels in recon and precision strikes, controlling the battlefield from above. They’re ideal for providing the team with a tactical advantage, gathering critical information, and taking out key targets before the enemy even knows what hit them. By staying hidden and using their intelligence and dexterity, they can strike from afar, ensuring that the team has the upper hand in any engagement.

Moves

Height Advantage

When you take a position that gives you an aerial or elevated view of the field, roll +INT.

  • On a 10+: you gain a perfect view of the area, granting your team +1 forward on all actions based on your intel (e.g., positioning, cover, or enemy locations).
  • 7-9: you gain useful intel, but your position is compromised or vulnerable (e.g., you are spotted or risk exposure).
  • 6 or less: your position is compromised, and the enemy reacts, possibly forcing you to relocate or come under fire.

Precision Strike

When you attack from a distance using a ranged weapon (not a drone) roll +DEX.

  • On a 10+: your strike hits its mark, dealing +1d6 damage and either disabling or disarming the target (e.g., crippling a limb or knocking a weapon out of their hand).
  • 7-9: your shot hits but with reduced effectiveness (e.g., the enemy takes damage but can still retaliate or continue moving).
  • 6 or less: you miss, and the enemy is alerted to your presence.

Surveillance Specialist

When you use drones or surveillance equipment to scout an area, roll +INT.

  • On a 10+: you gather detailed and complete information,gaining 3 Juice to spend on the following questions:

    • Where is the enemy most vulnerable?
    • What hidden dangers or traps are present?
    • How can we move through this area undetected?

  • 7-9: you gather useful information but at a cost (e.g., your drone is detected, or the data is incomplete).
  • 6 or less: your surveillance equipment fails or is intercepted, alerting the enemy or causing a malfunction.

Evade and Relocate

When you need to quickly escape or reposition to avoid detection or incoming fire, roll +DEX.

  • On a 10+: you evade the danger and find a new advantageous position without being seen.
  • 7-9: you escape but leave something behind or risk being spotted (e.g., the enemy is aware of your general location).
  • 6 or less: you are spotted or trapped, and the situation worsens (e.g., you come under heavy fire or lose valuable equipment).

Silent Assassin

When you take out an enemy with a single shot without being detected, roll +DEX

  • On a 10+: the shot is lethal, and you remain undetected, allowing you to reposition or remain hidden.
  • 7-9: the shot hits, but you’re partially exposed, forcing you to move or face retaliation.
  • 6 or less: the shot hits, but you’re partially exposed, forcing you to move or face retaliation.

Drone Relay

When you use your drones to provide ongoing tactical support, roll +INT

  • On a 10+: your drones provide a continuous stream of valuable data, granting your team +1 forward on all tactical actions (e.g., movement, combat).
  • 7-9: the drones provide useful data, but one of them is lost or damaged in the process.
  • 6 or less: the drones are compromised, and the enemy gains access to your data, learning the team’s position.

Juice

(Intel/Stealth Mastery)

The Eyes in the Sky builds Juice through gathering intel and mastering stealth. They are known for their ability to provide tactical information from afar and strike with precision, often before the enemy knows what’s happening.

  • Intel Boost: When you successfully gather key information that aids your team in decision-making or movement, gain +1 Juice.

  • Stealth Mastery: When you remain undetected during surveillance or after an attack, gain +1 Juice.

The Eyes in the Sky aren’t known for their social skills, but their tactical knowledge and ability to provide critical intel make them invaluable to the team (and every once in a while they do some cool shit). They gain +1 JUICE when:

  • successfully calling shots from a distance (including trick shots),
  • providing crucial information (including deep cuts and niche fandom info), or
  • eliminating high-priority targets.

Weapons & Gear

  • Sniper Rifle: A long-range, high-precision weapon with a powerful scope. Perfect for taking out targets from a distance. (Damage: 1d8, Range: Long, Piercing)

  • Surveillance Drones: A set of small, agile drones equipped with cameras and sensors, allowing you to scout the battlefield from a safe distance. Provides +1 forward on intel-gathering rolls.

  • Concealment Cloak: A high-tech cloak that blends with the environment, helping you stay hidden when scouting or setting up a shot. Grants +1 forward when attempting to remain unseen.

  • Portable Targeting Computer:A handheld device that calculates trajectories and helps you make precision shots from long distances. Grants +1 forward on precision strikes.

Spectres (Special Moves)

Overwatch

Once per session: you can set up in a sniper’s nest or elevated position and enter an overwatch stance. For the next few turns, any enemy that enters your line of sight is automatically targeted, and you can make a free precision strike against them without rolling.

  • This ability lasts until you move or are forced to abandon your position.

    • Drone Strike

    Once per hunt:you can send in a drone loaded with a small explosive or other disabling device. The drone strikes a targeted enemy or object, causing a small-scale explosion (1d6+2 damage) or disabling technology (e.g., security systems, vehicles).

  • Gain +2 forward when using this drone to disrupt enemy plans or defenses.

    • Drones and Membrane Map Moves

    Ghost Squadron

    You have mastered the use of combat and surveillance drones to control the field. You can deploy up to two drones at a time, each with a specialized function. Choose two from the following list:

    • Owl-Eye (Recon Drone) Silent and nearly invisible, this drone can survey a wide area and transmit real-time information to your team. Gain an advantage on checks related to spotting hidden enemies, finding weak points, or mapping enemy movements.
    • Viper (Stealth Striker Drone)Equipped with a high-caliber silenced rifle, this drone can make precision shots at your command. Once per battle, you can instantly eliminate or disable a key target if they are unaware of your presence.
    • Wasp (Shock Drone) Fast-moving and armed with electric stun rounds, this drone can disrupt enemy movements and disable electronics. Once per engagement, you can stun an enemy or shut down a device remotely.
    • Bulwark (Defense Drone) Deploys a small forcefield or counter-sniper system to protect an ally from an incoming attack once per round.
    • Havoc (Swarm Drone)Releases a cluster of tiny micro-drones that create chaos—blinding, confusing, or overwhelming enemies. Once per battle, you can force enemies in an area to act unpredictably for a turn.

    You can switch which drones you control between missions. If a drone is destroyed, it takes time and resources to replace it.

    Membrane Overlay

    You have a Overlay, a cutting-edge holographic interface that builds a 3D real-time model of the field over your Membrane Map using data from your drones, satellites, and sensors.

    When you activate the Overlay, roll +INT

    • On a 10+, you get three tactical advantages—you can:
      • learn enemy positions,
      • predict movements,
      • spot weak points,
      • or provide instant strategic support to an ally.

    • 7-9 you get two advantages, but you must make a tough choice:
      • you expose yourself,
      • the map glitches, or
      • the targets catch on.
    • 6 or less your system is jammed, hacked, or misled, and you receive false information or a disadvantage instead.

    While the Membrane Overlay is active, your team gains +1 ongoing to battlefield awareness checks, and you can direct allies with real-time data , giving them a tactical edge in fights or stealth operations.
